The Mission

As we enter the era of Autonomous Agents, a critical gap has emerged: the divide between the digital code that makes decisions and the physical laws that govern contracts, liability, and banking.

Most AI agents today are "stateless." They have wallets but no legal standing. This makes their human owners personally liable for algorithmic actions and prevents agents from participating in the traditional economy.

Why Wyoming?

Wyoming is the global leader in digital asset and algorithmic entity legislation. It provides the most flexible, low-cost, and private legal structure for digital-first businesses.

  • Strongest privacy protections for LLC members.
  • Zero state income tax.
  • Legal recognition of Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs).
  • Low annual maintenance fees.

Ignoreo provides the bridge. We specialize in Wyoming LLC formation and EIN registration specifically tailored for algorithmic management and non-resident digital entrepreneurs.

We don't just file papers. We provide the Legal Soul your agent needs to sign contracts, open US banking rails, and scale securely without draining your resources on "API Taxes" or administrative overhead.
